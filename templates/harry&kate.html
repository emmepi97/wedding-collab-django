How it all began

Our Story

It started with a glance across a crowded room in Florence — a Saturday evening in October, a shared table at a dinner party, and a conversation that lasted well past midnight.

What followed was three years of laughter, adventures across four continents, quiet Sunday mornings over coffee, and the growing certainty that this was it — the person you choose again and again.

On a rainy Tuesday in Paris, Harry got down on one knee at Café de Flore — Kate's favourite table by the window — and asked the question she had been hoping for since that very first evening in Florence.

She said yes before he finished the sentence.

Getting here

Travel & Accommodation

✈️ By Plane

The nearest airport is Rome Fiumicino (FCO), approximately 40 minutes from the venue. Direct flights available from most major European cities.

🚆 By Train

Rome Termini is the main railway hub. From there, take a taxi (approx. 20 min) or hop on our complimentary shuttle to the venue.

🚌 Shuttle Service

Complimentary shuttles depart from Hotel Artemide at 2:00 PM and return at 1:30 AM. Please confirm your seat in your RSVP.

🏨 Suggested Hotels

  • Hotel Artemide — 4★, Via Nazionale 22
    Special rate: mention "Kate & Harry Wedding"
  • Hotel dei Borgognoni — 4★, Via del Bufalo 126
  • Daphne Inn — Boutique, Via degli Avignonesi

What to wear

Dress Code

Black Tie Optional

We invite you to dress beautifully for a garden evening celebration. Formal attire is warmly welcomed — think elegant gowns, cocktail dresses, dark suits, and tuxedos.

Ladies

Floor-length gown, cocktail dress, or elegant midi. Heels or elegant flats are recommended — the ceremony takes place on a garden lawn.

Gentlemen

Dark suit, lounge suit, or tuxedo. A tie or pocket square adds a lovely touch to the evening.

Please avoid white, ivory, and cream — save those shades for the bride 🤍
