How it began

Our Story

✦

It started with a single glance across a crowded room in Florence — and neither of us quite looked away.

Emma and James first met at an architecture conference in the summer of 2019. James, a structural engineer, was presenting his research on sustainable design. Emma, a landscape architect, had arrived late and taken the last empty seat — which happened to be right next to him.

By the end of the afternoon they had missed two presentations, shared one espresso, and exchanged numbers on the back of a conference programme that Emma still keeps in her desk drawer.

Four years, countless adventures, and one very nervous question at sunset on the Amalfi Coast later — here we are.

Getting here

Travel & Accommodation

✦
✈

By Air

Nice Côte d'Azur Airport (NCE) is the closest airport, approximately 30 minutes from Cannes by taxi or the Navette Express bus (line 210).

Paris Charles de Gaulle (CDG) connects to Nice with flights every hour from most major European cities.

🚄

By Train

Cannes railway station (Gare de Cannes) is centrally located. TGV services connect to Paris in approximately 5.5 hours. Frequent regional TER trains run from Nice in 30 minutes.

Taxis and ride-hailing services are easily available from the station.
